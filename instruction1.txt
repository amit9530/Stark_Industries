Game instruction:

To open the game you must be registered.
The game will ask you if you are reegistered.
If you are, enter 'y' to go to the login screen.
If you are not, enter 'n' to go to registration screen.
You will be asked to choose category: 1 - school' 2 - indoor/home, 3- public places
After you chose a category, the game will begin.
A quuestion will apear with 3 answers, 
each answer will grant you diffrent points 0,1,2.
You can also skip the current quistion.
After you either chose your answers or skipped 5 questions, you will be graded accordingly.
Your grade will be save in the system data base.
