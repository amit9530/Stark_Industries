Game instruction:

You will be asked to choose category: 1 - school, 2 - home, 3- public places.
After you chose a category, the game will begin.
You will be presented with 5 questions, and for each question there will be 3 possible answers,
you need to choose the answer you think is right.
You can also skip the question, but it is limited to one skip per game.
After you finish the game, your grade will be calculated according to your answers.
